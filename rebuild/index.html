The New Site

It was time to revamp the site. I wanted something clean and simple, and thanks to the framework provided by Bootstrap, it was painless. Not only is Bootstrap easy to use, it provides a layout that is responsive and mobile friendly. (Go ahead, resize your window or flip your device!)

The focus of the site is now on my data analysis and visualization work. My academic research is well represented in other venues, such as Google Scholar and ResearchGate.

In addition to adding new projects, I will start posting articles on the things I've learned and what I see at the intersection of science and data visualization.
